osu!FRDG is an osu! skin for the standard/taiko gamemodes. 



This skin features my own dark, minimalistic design with a focus on no distractions while maintaining 
good looking gameplay.



UI SFX were sampled from the SID Expansion Pack for the ReFX Nexus VST plugin. osu!standard hitsounds
were taken from the Argon skin for osu!Lazer, and the osu!taiko hitsounds are samples of the Amen Break
that I've EQ'd to make them sound satisfying to play with.
